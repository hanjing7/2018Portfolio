Data Analysis & Visualization

Air Crash Challengies

Data Analysis & Visualization

According to the requirements in Airport Design Challenge, the project plans to take an in-depth look at all historical air crash accident and analyze the possible causes of all those tragedies. The overall objective of this project is to help identify potential security leaks existing in airport management and aircraft routing by discovering connection among all causes, either direct or indirect.

    Data Comparisons

- Comparison among No. of crashes and the fatality of crushes by location or airport
- Comparing the ratio between fatality rates and overall fatalities for private flights and commercial flights
- Attribute detailed accident causes to big categories to learn leading causes of those accidents
- Relate the causes with aircraft damage trend.
- Comparing accident by flight detail, like category, engine, maker and so forth.

  • Type: Course / Competition Project
  • Role: Data Analysis & Processing, Visualization Design
  • Year: 2014
